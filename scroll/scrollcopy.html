Timeline

  • 1951 - Keep America Beautiful is formed by the Beverage Industry. Anti-Litter sentiments begin.
  • 1969 - The Plastics Industry was aware of the upcoming waste crisis and prepared for public backlash
  • 1971 - The infamous Crying Indian ad by K.A.B airs as a PSA on public television.The recycling movement takes off
  • 1978 - Coca-Cola and Pepsi start selling drinks in plastic bottles rather than deposit glass containers.
  • 2015 - The American Plastic Bag Association pass preemption laws to prevent states from banning plastic bags.
  • 2018 - Industry giants form the End Plastic Waste Alliance, $1.5 bn USD is pledged to solve the issue with recycling. However, members Shell and Exxon invest $180bn into continued production for plastic.
